School Supplies!

School Supplies

● Laptop with working camera/microphone and charger (Note that students are expected to charge devices overnight and come to school with those fully charged for the day. Students should bring a charger each day.)

● Headphones with a microphone (These should be with students for all classes.)

● Printer (All students should have printing capabilities at home.)

● Scanner (This can be through a printer or PDF Scanner; having this on an accessible phone is acceptable.)

● Calculator – LABELED WITH YOUR NAME

● Algebra 2 or above: TI-83 or 84 graphing calculator

● All other students: Scientific calculator

● Student day planner (This is required for all middle school students, but recommended for all high school students.)

● Binders as required by individual teachers

● Package of binder divider tabs for each binder as required by individual teachers

● Loose notebook paper with reinforced holes in each binder

● Zippered binder pencil pouch

● Set of blue or black pens

● Plenty of #2 pencils and a small manual pencil sharpener

● Marble, lined composition notebooks as required by individual teachers

● Spiral 1-subject notebooks as required by individual teachers

● 3-ring hole puncher (This should be kept at home for use there.)
